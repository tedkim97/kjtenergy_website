ABOUT

KJT Renewable LLC is a solar-focused, renewable energy consulting firm based in Chicago, IL. The founder, Dr. Taesung Kim has 32 years of industrial experience in all aspects of renewable energy engineering - including research, six sigma level quality manufacturing, and executive leadership. Taesung's career started in the semiconductor industry and evolved into Opto-electronic Semiconductors. In the past 18 years, he has actively led businesses in executing design, EPC, operation and quality control, worldwide marketing, and outsourcing of critical technologies through forming strategic alliances in the renewable energy, such as Solar PV as well as Wind energy. He has performed technical due diligence on utility scale power plant projects including site visits such as about 1GWdc of Solar PV power plant projects, 3GWac Wind power plant projects, 1GWac of Hydro power plant projects, 760MWac Natural Gas power plant project, 6MWac Biomass power plant project, 1.6MWh ESS project combined with 3MWdc PV in the USA, India, Brazil, Europe, and South Korea including 5MWac microgrid in Sierra Leon in Africa and 10MWac Geothermal power plant in Turkey.
