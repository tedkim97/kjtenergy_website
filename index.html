About

KJT Renewable LLC is a energy consulting company based in Chicago, IL. The founder, Dr. Taesung Kim has 32 years of industrial experience in all aspects of renewable energy engineering - from research to six sigma level quality manufacturing to executive leadership. Taesung Kim started in the semiconductor industry, beginning his career in Opto-electronic Semiconductors. In the past 18 years, he has actively led businesses in executing design, EPC, operation and quality control, worldwide marketing and outsourcing of critical technologies through forming strategic alliances in the renewable energy, such as Solar PV as well as Wind energy.

Consulting

We currently consult on projects relating relating to Mid to Utility scale solar PV power plants. Our consulting includes several aspects of a project such as:
  • Site Surveys and Acquisitions
  • Geotechnical Studies and EIA
  • Zoning (SUP or CUP)
  • PBR
  • Interconnections
  • EPC
  • O&M
  • Financial
  • Tax Equity
