About

The CEO, Dr.Taesung Kim, has extensive career experience in all aspects of engineering from research to six sigma level quality manufacturing and has 32 years of industrial experience including executive leadership. His career began in Semiconductors, and then Optoelectronic Semiconductors including LED, LD, and Solar cells. In the past 18 years, he has actively led businesses in executing design, EPC, operation, and six sigma level quality control, worldwide marketing, and outsourcing of critical technologies through forming strategic alliances in the renewable energy industry, including Solar PV and Wind energy. In addition to his project experience below, he has collaborated and researched the potential launch of a digital cryptocurrency for renewable energy power projects.

Project Experience: 10GWp Solar PV, 10GWac Wind, 5GWh BESS, 727MWp Natural Gas, and 5.5MWp Small Hydro power plants in India, Brazil, USA, and Korea
